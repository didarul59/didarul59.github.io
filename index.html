ABOUT ME

Hi! Welcome to My Site!

I have recently completed my B.Sc. degree in Theoretical Physics at the Shahjalal University Of Science And Technology, Bangladesh. My undergraduate project was on investigating the electronic properties of transitional metal oxide using density functional theory in the Quantum ESPRESSO packages under the supervision of Dr. Enamul Haque (Associate Professor, Department of Physics, SUST).

My current research/project: I am currently working as a research assistant in Computational Material Science at the High-Performance Computing Lab (HPC Lab) of the Department of Physics, SUST. My current project involves utilizing time-dependent density functional theory to evaluate the optical properties of a metal oxide and comparing the result with experimental data obtained from the Laser Ablation method. The experiment was conducted at the Non-linear Optics Lab at SUST.
